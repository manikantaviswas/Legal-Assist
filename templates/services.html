Our Services

LegalAssist FIR provides cutting-edge AI solutions to help law enforcement officials accurately record and process First Information Reports. Our services are designed to improve the efficiency and accuracy of legal documentation in police stations.

AI-Powered FIR Section Analysis

AI-Powered FIR Section Analysis

Our core service uses advanced natural language processing to analyze complaint details and suggest appropriate legal sections and acts that should be included in the FIR.

Features:

  • Instant section identification based on case details
  • Multi-language support for regional languages
  • Explanation of why specific sections apply
  • Continuous learning from verified case outcomes
  • Works with both typed and spoken input
FIR Template Generation

FIR Template Generation

Based on the complaint details and identified legal sections, our system generates properly formatted FIR templates with all necessary fields pre-filled, saving time and reducing administrative errors.

Features:

  • Auto-populated FIR formats with identified sections
  • Standardized language for legal clarity
  • Digital export options (PDF, document formats)
  • Integration with existing police record systems
  • Version history and audit trails
