About LegalAssist FIR

Our Mission

LegalAssist FIR was developed with a clear purpose: to empower police officers with instant access to accurate legal information when registering First Information Reports (FIRs).

We understand the challenges faced by police stations that lack dedicated legal experts. Our AI-powered solution bridges this gap by providing reliable, instant guidance on applicable sections and acts based on case details.

By ensuring accurate legal documentation from the start, we help strengthen the foundation of investigations and support the delivery of justice.

Who We Are

LegalAssist FIR brings together expertise from law enforcement professionals, legal experts, and AI specialists. Our team has worked closely with police departments to understand the specific challenges faced during the FIR registration process.

Our solution is built on a comprehensive database of Indian legal codes, sections, and precedents, all made accessible through an intuitive interface designed specifically for police officers working in high-pressure environments.

We continuously update our legal database to incorporate amendments and new precedents, ensuring that the information provided is always current and reliable.
