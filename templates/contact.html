Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the size of your department and existing systems. This includes installation, staff training, and customization to your specific requirements.

Is the system available in regional languages?

Yes, LegalAssist FIR supports 12 major Indian languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, and others. We can add additional language support based on your requirements.

How frequently is the legal database updated?

Our legal database is updated on a weekly basis to incorporate new amendments, notifications, and landmark judgments. Critical updates are pushed immediately as they occur.

Can we integrate this with our existing case management system?

Yes, LegalAssist FIR is designed with API capabilities that allow integration with most common case management systems used by police departments. Our technical team will work with you to ensure smooth integration.
