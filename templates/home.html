MISSION

To uphold the law and maintain public order through professional policing; to ensure safety and security of all citizens; to prevent and detect crime through intelligence-led policing; and to leverage technology for efficient and effective law enforcement across Telangana State.

DGP's Message

Telangana Police is committed to being a citizen-friendly force that ensures the safety and security of all residents. With modern technology and community policing initiatives, we strive to maintain law and order while building trust with the public we serve.
